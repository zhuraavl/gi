Departement de la Haute — Savoie

New buildings on the Montigny business park in Montigny à Maxilly. Red, green and gray, these buildings will not only shelter craft activities but also ap-partments.

On the ground floor will be the offices, the first floor storage space and the second apartment (with balconies) of the craftsman and his family.

But the latter can also choose to stay on two levels if he does not want to live on site. 124 parking spaces will be developed.

With panoramic views of the lake.

And so that families who have chosen to settle there forget the fact of living in an area of activities, Igor Iline has treated the architecture. "I do not like corrugated siding and homogeneous areas. The buildings will be colorful and the walls thick.
